TaskBreakdown: Your SMART Goal Automation Assistant

Streamline your goals into actionable plans using AI-driven productivity tools.

TaskBreakdown is a productivity tool designed to transform your ambitions into actionable, achievable plans. Built for the Gemini Developer Competition, this application combines advanced prompt engineering with function calling to provide an intelligent, streamlined approach to goal-setting and execution.

Features & Functionality:

  • SMART Goal Automation: Whether you have a detailed goal in mind or just a vague idea, TaskBreakdown can guide you. If a SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound) goal isn’t provided, the app walks you through creating one. From there, it generates a personalized, detailed action plan with timelines and milestones.
    • Example: I used this tool to prepare for my 10K run with a target time of 65 minutes. TaskBreakdown generated a structured plan, syncing milestones with my Google Calendar. I completed the run at 68 minutes—just shy of my goal, but the plan kept me motivated and on track.
  • Google Suite Integration: With Google OAuth authentication, the app seamlessly syncs tasks and deadlines to Google Calendar, ensuring your plans are always accessible and progress is tracked.
  • Streamlined Interface: Developed with Streamlit, the user-friendly interface makes planning intuitive and engaging. It guides users through goal creation and task breakdown while ensuring ease of use.
  • Advanced API Management: Powered by Gemini API, TaskBreakdown employs rate limit mechanisms to optimize API calls and traffic, ensuring smooth operation even during high usage.
